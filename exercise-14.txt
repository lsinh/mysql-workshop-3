Reverse all fax phone numbers, keeping the phone numbers in
a valid format

List all of the new fax phone number values

mysql> SELECT content,  CONCAT('(',  SUBSTRING(REVERSE(content),1,3),  ')', ' ', 
substring(reverse(content),4,1),  substring(reverse(content),6,2), ('-'), 
substring(reverse(content),8,1), substring(reverse(content),11,3))  
AS reversed FROM Phone WHERE SUBTYPE='fax' AND content LIKE '(%' limit 50;
